Player Expectations

Our Expectations

  • Scheduled Practices

    Starters are expected to make all scheduled practices. We have team practice 4 days a week: Sunday, Monday, and twice before default match days. Each session lasts approximately 2-3 hours. Any excused absences must be posted in the team Discord by the day of practice.

  • Individual Playtime

    Players need to be playing outside of practice and matches to maintain and improve individual skill. This should be through playing FACEIT, Premier, or Matchmaking. Late Night and 10-Mans do NOT count towards this requirement. A suggested guideline is at least 3-5 competitive matches per week.

  • Performance & Self-Awareness

    Players are expected to put in extra time if they're not performing. This refers to a noticeable drop in your HLTV Rating and overall underperformance in matches. This isn't something the team will track for you; you should be self-aware of your performance and take the initiative to improve. This extra time should focus on areas where you're struggling, including VOD reviews, Refrag, or other targeted training.

  • Accountability

    If starters are not consistently meeting these requirements, benching could be an outcome. The duration of benching will be handled on a case-by-case basis, with the aim of helping the player improve and meet team standards.

  • Team Communication

    Team communications need to be clear and concise. We expect clear voice comms in Discord during matches and practice. Be honest and proactive with feedback; the earlier things can change during a match, the better for the team.

  • Performance Tracking

    We'll be using CSStats.gg to track ESEA Match Performance, specifically focusing on the HLTV rating for individual insights.
