## Match Overview

SaltcrewGG came into this match prepared and focused, delivering one of our strongest performances this season. Our preparation on Dust2 paid off as we executed our game plan flawlessly, securing a 16-12 victory against Team Alpha.

## First Half (T-Side)

Starting on the terrorist side, we came out aggressive with a strong pistol round win. Our mid-round calling was exceptional, with multiple successful B splits that caught Alpha off-guard.

**Key Highlights:**
- Won pistol round with a well-executed B rush
- FuzzyHat dropped 12 kills in the first half alone
- Successfully executed 4 out of 5 planned B site takes
- Ended the half 9-6

## Second Half (CT-Side)

The second half saw us switch to CT-side where we faced increased pressure from Alpha's T-side. They came out strong with an early pistol round win, but we quickly regained momentum.

**Defensive Standouts:**
- h0t_s0up's AWP holds on long A were crucial, securing 8 kills
- GUNK anchored B site perfectly, denying multiple rushes
- Won the eco round (Round 18) which broke their economy

## Clutch Moments

Round 23 was the turning point. Down 11-12, kiwi pulled off a 1v2 clutch defuse that swung the momentum completely in our favor. We rode that wave to close out the remaining rounds.

## Individual Performance

- **FuzzyHat**: 24-15 K/D, 1.45 HLTV Rating - MVP performance
- **h0t_s0up**: 21-17 K/D, exceptional AWP performance
- **GUNK**: 18-16 K/D, solid site anchor
- **kiwi**: 19-18 K/D, clutch plays when it mattered
- **AlexBG**: 16-14 K/D, great support play

## What Worked

1. **Communication** - Our calls were clear and timely throughout the match
2. **Utility Usage** - Excellent smoke and flash coordination on executes
3. **Trading** - We had a 78% trade kill success rate
4. **Discipline** - Minimal overpeeking and unnecessary risks

## Areas for Improvement

While the win was convincing, we identified some areas to work on:

- CT-side pistol rounds need more practice
- Mid control was contested too easily
- Late-round rotations could be faster

## Looking Ahead

This match demonstrated that when we execute our strategies and communicate effectively, we can compete with anyone in our division. We'll carry this momentum into our next match while addressing the areas that need work.
